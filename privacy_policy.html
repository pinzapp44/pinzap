Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 14, 2026

Summary: Pinzap stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

1. Introduction

Pinzap ("we", "our", or "the app") is a location-based automation app that alerts you when you enter or exit zones you define. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, or any account information
  • We do not collect or transmit your location data to any server
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting services
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking
  • We do not have a backend server

3. Location Data

Pinzap requires access to your device's location to function. Here is how location data is used:

  • On-device only: Your location is processed entirely on your device and is never sent to any external server
  • Background location: The app accesses location in the background to detect when you enter or exit zones you have defined
  • Zone coordinates: The locations of your saved zones are stored locally in your device's storage using AsyncStorage
  • No logging: We do not log, store, or analyse your location history

4. Data Storage

All app data — including your saved zones, settings, and preferences — is stored locally on your device using Android's AsyncStorage. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the app
  • Is not backed up to any cloud service by Pinzap

5. Permissions Used

  • ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION / ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION: To show your position on the map and detect nearby zones
  • ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION: To detect zones when the app is in the background or closed
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To send zone entry/exit alerts
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE: To keep the GPS active in the background
  • VIBRATE / WAKE_LOCK: To trigger alarm vibration when you arrive at a zone
  • INTERNET: To load map tiles from OpenStreetMap (no personal data sent)

6. Third-Party Services

Pinzap uses the following third-party services solely for map display:

  • OpenStreetMap (tile.openstreetmap.org) — map tile images. Only your approximate viewport coordinates are sent to load map tiles, which is standard behaviour for all mapping apps. See OpenStreetMap's Privacy Policy.
  • Nominatim (nominatim.openstreetmap.org) — location search. Your search query text is sent when you search for a location.

No other third-party services, analytics, or advertising networks are used.

7. Children's Privacy

Pinzap is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
