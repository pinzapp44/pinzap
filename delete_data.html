Pinzap — Data Deletion Request

Good news: Pinzap stores all data locally on your device only. There is no server, no account, and no cloud storage. Uninstalling the app deletes all your data instantly and permanently.

How to delete all your data

Option 1 — Uninstall the app (recommended)

  1. Long press the Pinzap icon on your home screen
  2. Tap Uninstall
  3. Confirm — all zones, settings and data are permanently deleted

Option 2 — Clear app data (keeps app installed)

  1. Open Settings on your phone
  2. Tap Apps → find Pinzap
  3. Tap Storage
  4. Tap Clear Data
  5. Confirm — all zones and settings are deleted

What data is deleted

  • All saved zones (names, coordinates, radius)
  • All zone actions and reminder messages
  • App settings and preferences
  • Permission status records

What data is NOT stored

  • No account or personal information — Pinzap has no login
  • No location history — location is only used in real-time
  • No data on any server — everything is on your device only
